About Us

Welcome to Handcrafts by Mariam, where we specialize in creating unique and glossy handmade resin art. Each piece is carefully crafted with love and attention to detail, making them perfect for gifting or adding a touch of beauty to your life. We offer a variety of products, including necklaces, earrings, and decorative items, all made from high-quality resin.

At Handcrafts by Mariam, we believe in the power of handmade creations to bring joy and meaning. Our custom order service allows you to personalize your items, ensuring that each piece is as unique as you are.
